## Tangent

- C++, DX11

Single-player racing game made in about a week. Utilizes the geometry shader to create geometry based on a BÃ©zier curve. Customizable at runtime (segment/iteration count, radius)

Custom post-processing effects:
 - Bloom (three pass effect; first a multi-pass Gaussian blur, then a high pass filter, followed by a combination pass)
 - Grayscale
 - Simple blur
